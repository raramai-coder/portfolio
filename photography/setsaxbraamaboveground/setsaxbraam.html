Setsa x Braam Above Ground

If you’re looking for charisma, Setsa is the guy to go to. If you’re for character Braamfontein is the city to go to. Now you put these two together, and it’s a wonderful entanglement of charisma and character. For this photoshoot I decided to capture the city from rooftops and low-angles, giving the viewer an experience of Braam above the ground.
