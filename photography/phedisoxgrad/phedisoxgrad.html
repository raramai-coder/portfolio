Phediso x Grad

Graduating from university is no easy feat, but Phediso did it all while proving that pink really is the superior colour! This night-time photoshoot focuses less on displaying the large parts of University of Witwatersrand architecture, but more on showcasing the small details that make it a stunning backdrop for photoshoots. Owing to the flash photography technique used, Phediso is centralised in all images and made the focus by virtue of the background being in the shadows, which is a good thing because she looked breath-taking, and these photos ought to immortalise that!

Model: Phediso L

Location: University of Witwatersrand
